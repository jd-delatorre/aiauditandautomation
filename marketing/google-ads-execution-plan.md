# NJ AI Automation — Google Ads Execution Plan

## Important: Why NOT Google Local Services Ads

Google Local Services Ads (LSA) does **not** support consulting categories. The ~100 eligible categories cover home services (plumbing, electrical, HVAC), regulated professions (lawyers, accountants, real estate agents), and personal services (tutors, pet groomers). "AI consultant," "business consultant," "management consultant," and "IT consultant" are all explicitly absent.

**Do not waste time applying for LSA.** Instead, Google Search Ads is the right channel — it gives you superior targeting, full creative control, and conversion tracking for B2B consulting.

## Optimization Playbook

### Week 1–2: Launch & Learn
- Launch all 4 campaigns
- Set daily budgets (monthly / 30)
- Enable all ad extensions
- Let Google's algorithm gather data
- Do NOT make changes yet

### Week 3–4: First Optimization
- Review Search Terms Report — add irrelevant terms to negative keyword list
- Pause keywords with 100+ impressions and 0 clicks
- Identify top 3 performing keywords per campaign
- A/B test: pause lowest-performing ad variation, let winner run

### Month 2: Reallocation
- Move 10–15% of budget from underperforming campaigns to top performers
- Add new keyword variations based on search term discoveries
- Refresh ad copy for fatigued ads (CTR declining)
- Check conversion tracking — ensure all form submissions and calls are tracked

### Month 3+: Scale What Works
- Double down on campaigns with lowest cost-per-lead
- Create new ad groups for high-performing keyword clusters
- Build remarketing audiences from website visitors (for future Display campaigns)
- Consider expanding to Bing Ads using your best-performing Google campaigns

## Conversion Tracking Setup (Critical)

You MUST set up conversion tracking before launching. Without it, you're flying blind.

### Track These Events:
1. **Discovery call booked** (Calendly thank-you page or form submission)
2. **Phone calls** (Google call tracking or CallRail)
3. **Contact form submissions** (thank-you page redirect)
4. **Time on landing page > 2 minutes** (engagement signal)

### Tools:
- Google Ads conversion tracking tag on thank-you pages
- Google Tag Manager for event tracking
- Google Analytics 4 linked to Google Ads
- Optional: CallRail ($45/month) for phone call attribution
