## Notes on Using These Emails

### Who These Work Best For
- Business owners who've heard of AI but aren't sure it applies to them
- People skeptical of tech hype who respond better to plain-language explanations
- Warm leads or referrals who need education before a pitch
- Anyone who responded neutrally to a more data-heavy email

### Tone Reminders
- Keep the friendly, conversational energy — these aren't cold blasts, they're warm introductions
- Personalize [Company Name] and [First Name] before every send
- Don't over-explain the tech — the goal is to make them curious, not give them a manual

### Suggested Pairing
These emails work well as:
- A follow-up to someone who engaged with a social post but hasn't booked a call
- A second-touch email after a first email with no reply
- A warm intro for referrals who need a soft entry point
