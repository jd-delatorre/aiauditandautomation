Section 02

Current State Assessment

The following assessment is based on 2.5 hours of on-site observation and interviews with {{CONTACT_NAME}} ({{CONTACT_TITLE}}) and three additional team members at {{CLIENT_NAME}}.

Communication & Response Times

Client inquiries arrive via email, WhatsApp, phone calls, and social media DMs. There is no centralized inbox ‚Äî each team member monitors their own channels. New lead inquiries received after 5 PM typically go unanswered until the following business day, with average first-response times of 4-6 hours during business hours.

Observation

During our 2.5-hour observation window, we counted 14 incoming client messages. Of these, 9 were routine inquiries (pricing, availability, status updates) that could be handled by an AI assistant with pre-configured responses.

Scheduling & Calendar Management

Appointments are booked through a combination of email threads and phone calls. No online booking system is in use. Team members manually check availability across multiple calendars before confirming times. Reminder calls are made manually the day before each appointment.

Observation

Two team members spend an estimated 45 minutes daily coordinating schedules. No-show rate is approximately 12%, with no automated reminder system in place.

Email & Document Handling

The team processes approximately 80-120 emails daily across all accounts. Email triage is manual ‚Äî each team member scans their inbox for priority items. Weekly performance reports are compiled manually from multiple data sources, taking approximately 5 hours per report.

Observation

We observed significant time spent on repetitive email patterns: status update requests, document collection emails, and meeting follow-ups. Over 60% of outbound emails follow predictable templates.

Repetitive Administrative Tasks

Daily and weekly administrative tasks include data entry between systems, generating client-facing reports, invoice preparation, and tracking project milestones. These tasks are performed manually with minimal use of automation tools or templates.

Data Entry & Reporting

Information is frequently transferred manually between platforms ‚Äî from CRM to spreadsheets, from email to project management tools, and from analytics dashboards to client reports. This introduces both time waste and error potential.

Glossary

AI (Artificial Intelligence)

Software that can understand language, make decisions, and perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

LLM (Large Language Model)

The AI "brain" that powers understanding and generation of text. Examples include Claude (Anthropic) and GPT (OpenAI).

Self-Hosted

Software that runs on servers you control, rather than on a third party's cloud infrastructure. Provides data privacy and ownership.

API (Application Programming Interface)

A connection that allows different software systems to communicate with each other. Used to connect AI to your existing tools.

OpenClaw

An open-source, self-hosted AI assistant platform that connects to messaging apps, email, browsers, and more to automate business tasks.

VPS (Virtual Private Server)

A dedicated cloud computer that runs your AI assistant 24/7. Typically costs $5-20/month.
