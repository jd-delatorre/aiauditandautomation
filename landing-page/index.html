Not at all. We handle everything technical. You just need to show us how your business operates day-to-day.
Is my business data safe with AI? expand_more
Yes. We use self-hosted AI that runs on your own infrastructure. Your data never leaves your servers. No third-party access.
What if the audit doesn't find anything useful? expand_more
In our experience, every business with 10+ employees has significant automation opportunities. But if you're not satisfied, we'll make it right.
What happens after the audit? expand_more
You receive a written report with 5–15 opportunities and ROI estimates. If you want to move forward, we implement the automations. If not, you keep the report — no pressure.
