## WHAT THIS IS NOT (3:00 - 3:45)

**[John on camera]**

Now, I want to be upfront about what this is NOT — because it's not magic, and it's not right for every business.

**The automations aren't plug-and-play.**
They require setup and configuration. That's where I come in — I handle all of that for you. That's why we start with the audit, so we know exactly what to build.

**It's not a replacement for human judgment.**
It's an assistant, not a decision-maker. For important stuff, it drafts and you approve.

**It's not free to run.**
The software is free, but you'll pay for the AI — usually $20-100 a month depending on how much you use it. Plus a few bucks for hosting. I'm always transparent about that.

**It's not a website chatbot.**
This isn't the little bubble in the corner of a website. This is a back-office tool that works through WhatsApp, Slack, email — wherever you actually communicate.

And **it's not for mega-scale operations.**
If you're handling 10,000 customer chats a day, you need enterprise software. This is ideal for small to mid-size businesses doing 10 to a few hundred interactions daily.
